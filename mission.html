Mission & Vision

The JKCS Peer Tutoring Program is a senior-led initiative that aspires to cultivate a collaborative learning environment for our partners, volunteers and community.

Mission

To foster maturity and mentorship in our student role models and academically enrich John Knox through guidance and camaraderie.

Vision

Be a paradigm of student leadership that reflects John Knoxâ€™s Christ-centered values, and provides inspired individuals an opportunity to critically engage within their community and channel the enthusiasms they are passionate about.

Values

The JKCS Peer Tutoring Program was founded on the principles of:

  • Community - We strive to empower its driven leaders by providing them with the resources and opportunities to connect, engage and mentor their peers and junior students within their school community.

  • Individuality - The Peer Tutoring Program thrives on the diverse perspectives and ingenious solutions that each of the program's associates bring to the table. Individuality is essential to our ability to independently make decisions and connect with others in our own unique way.

  • responsibility - This initiative focuses on nurturing a sense of global responsibility in all parties involved and developing necessary interpersonal, communication, and administrative soft skills that can only be learned through experience and management.
