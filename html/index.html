About Us

The JKCS Peer Tutoring Program is a senior-led initiative founded in October of 2020 with the aim of promoting academic success through peer support. This initiative is free of charge and seeks the contribution of future-minded high school students that recognize the necessity of community, accountability and patience. We aspire to cultivate an environment that provides a sustainable approach to the well-being of our partners, volunteers and our community.
